Orlando Mobility Scooter Rentals Made Easy

Planning a trip to Orlando's world-famous theme parks? Scootz makes mobility scooter rentals simple, affordable, and hassle-free. Whether you're visiting Walt Disney World, Universal Orlando Resort, SeaWorld, or any of the area's amazing attractions, our theme park approved scooters ensure you can enjoy every moment without worrying about fatigue or mobility limitations.

Unlike the scooters available for rent inside the parks, which are first-come, first-served and must stay within park boundaries, a Scootz rental gives you the freedom to use your scooter throughout your entire vacation. Take it back to your hotel, use it at Disney Springs or CityWalk, and have it ready first thing in the morning—no waiting in lines or hoping one is available.

Why Choose Scootz for Your Rental?

Free Delivery & Pickup

We deliver directly to Disney resorts, Universal hotels, and vacation rentals within 18 miles—at no extra charge.

All-Day Battery Life

Our scooters offer 12-17+ miles per charge, providing 10-18 hours of park time on a single overnight charge.

Theme Park Approved

All our scooters meet Disney, Universal, and SeaWorld size requirements and are allowed on all park transportation.

Family-Owned Service

As a local family business, we're available 7 days a week to answer questions and provide personalized support.
