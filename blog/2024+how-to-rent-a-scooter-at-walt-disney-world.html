With so much to see and do, it's essential to have a convenient and comfortable means of transport if you have mobility challenges. Tips for Using Your Scooter at Walt Disney World

To make the most of your scooter rental at Walt Disney World, consider these helpful tips:

Familiarize Yourself with Park Maps

Before arriving at the parks, take some time to review the park maps and plan your route. This will help you identify accessible pathways, attractions, and rest areas, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable experience.

Charge Your Scooter Overnight

Each night, make sure to charge your scooter's battery to ensure you have enough power for a full day of fun. Most scooters can easily be charged using a standard power outlet, so you won't have to worry about running out of juice during your adventure.

Take Breaks and Stay Hydrated

With so much to see and do, it's easy to get caught up in the excitement of Walt Disney World. Remember to take breaks throughout the day and stay hydrated, especially during warm weather. Your scooter rental will provide a comfortable place to rest and recharge your energy.

Be Mindful of Other Guests

While using your scooter, be mindful of other guests around you. Maintain a safe speed and avoid sudden stops or sharp turns to ensure everyone's safety and enjoyment.
