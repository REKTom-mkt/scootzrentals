Tips for Using Your Scooter at Disney World

Plan Your Route

Disney World is expansive, so plan your route in advance to make the most of your time. Highlight the attractions you want to visit and map out the most efficient way to get there.

Take Breaks

Even with a scooter, it's important to take breaks and stay hydrated. Find shaded areas to rest and recharge.

Adhere to Park Rules

Follow Disney World's guidelines for scooter use to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for everyone. Stick to designated paths and be mindful of other visitors.

Utilize Charging Stations

Our scooters have a long battery life, but it's always a good idea to utilize charging stations throughout the park. Keep an eye on your battery level and recharge as needed.
