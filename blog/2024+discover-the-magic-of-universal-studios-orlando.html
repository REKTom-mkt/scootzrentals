Exploring Universal Studios Orlando with a Scooter

Universal Studios Orlando is divided into several themed areas, each offering unique attractions and experiences. Here’s a guide to making the most of your visit with a Scootz scooter:

1. Production Central:

  • Start your adventure in Production Central, home to attractions like Despicable Me Minion Mayhem and Shrek 4-D. Use your scooter to easily move between these fun-filled rides and shows.

2. New York:

  • Head to the New York section, where you can enjoy attractions like Revenge of the Mummy and Race Through New York Starring Jimmy Fallon. Scoot around the bustling streets and take in the sights without any discomfort.

3. Hollywood:

  • Explore Hollywood Boulevard, with attractions such as Universal's Horror Make-Up Show and the Hollywood Rip Ride Rockit roller coaster. Your scooter will make it easy to get to each exciting spot.

4. San Francisco:

  • Don’t miss the San Francisco area, featuring the thrilling Fast & Furious – Supercharged ride. Navigate through this area effortlessly with your scooter.

5. The Wizarding World of Harry Potter™ – Diagon Alley:

  • Immerse yourself in the magic of Diagon Alley, where you can experience attractions like Harry Potter and the Escape from Gringotts. Your scooter will allow you to explore every magical corner comfortably.

6. Springfield:

  • Visit Springfield, home to The Simpsons Ride and other whimsical attractions. Enjoy the themed area without worrying about mobility issues.

7. World Expo:

  • Discover the World Expo area, with rides like Men in Black Alien Attack. Effortlessly scoot from one attraction to the next.
