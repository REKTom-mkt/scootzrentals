Why Rent from Scootz

At Scootz, we’re not just your average mobility scooter rental service; we’re your trusted partners who can enhance your Orlando vacation experience! With our competitive pricing and free delivery, we make renting mobility scooters affordable with absolutely no hidden fees!

WHY WE'RE DIFFERENT

The Scootz

Our scooters are equipped with industry-leading technology, wrapped in innovative style and design.

Navigation

Navigating through tight que lines and crowded spaces? No problem! Our Scootz Portable is designed with zero turn steering so you can effortlessly maneuver through even the trickiest of spots.

Convenience

Convenience is our game. Looking for a lightweight and portable option? The Scootz Portable weighs in at only 46.5 lbs. Plus, without the need to remove the battery or disassemble the scooter it’s a breeze to transport. The Endurance and Revo 3/4 Wheel Scooters offer one click disassembly and re-assembly makes it effortless to transport in vehicles of all sizes. Our scooters will fit in cars of all makes, models and sizes including compact cars.

Park Approved

All of our scooters are theme park approved, ensuring you’re in compliance with all theme park regulations.

Battery Life

Battery life varies from 12+ to 17+ miles per charge. The Scootz Portable has a range of up 12+ miles which is the equivalent of up to 10-15+ hours in the parks. The Endurance offers a range of 14+ miles per charge, which is equivalent to 15-18 hours in park. The Scootz Revo offers a range of up to 17+ miles which is the equivalent of up to 15-18+ hours per charge. Provided they’re charged overnight and you begin the day with a full charge, you can get through the day on a single charge (rider weight and average speed may impact range).

All Day Every Day

Unlike the scooters available at Disney, Universal and Seaworld which are first come first serve. They must stay within the park, our service offers you the flexibility to have your scooter for the entire duration of your trip. Go where you want, when you want and have it when you need it the most!

Family Owned

We are an independently owned and operated family business that takes pride in being readily available for any questions or concerns, so we can best service all of your mobility needs!

All in All

Remember, we are the hassle free, transparent and customer driven mobility scooter rental service you're looking for. And let's not forget the technology, style and heart Scootz offers which will optimize your overall experience!
